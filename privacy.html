Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 1, 2025

Your Privacy at a Glance: Dial-Assist stores all your health data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal health information with external servers without your explicit consent.

1. Introduction

Precision Dialysis Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Dial-Assist mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

Dial-Assist is designed as a dialysis adherence tracking tool. Your privacy and the security of your health information are our highest priorities.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use Dial-Assist, you may provide the following information, which is stored locally on your device:

  • Personal information (name, date of birth)
  • Healthcare provider information (doctor, nurse, dialysis center, pharmacy contact details)
  • Dialysis treatment information (treatment type, schedule, prescribed procedures)
  • Session data (treatment times, completion status, notes)
  • Photos you choose to attach to sessions
  • Supply inventory and barcode scan data
  • Voice recordings for voice command functionality (processed locally, not stored)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect:

  • Device information (iOS version, device type)
  • App usage analytics (feature usage, session statistics) - stored locally only
  • Crash reports (anonymized, sent only with your permission)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide the App's functionality:

  • Track your dialysis treatment adherence
  • Provide voice-guided step-by-step instructions
  • Generate session reports and analytics
  • Manage dialysis supply inventory
  • Enable you to share reports with your healthcare providers (only when you explicitly choose to)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your health data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage (Core Data and encrypted UserDefaults). We do not have servers that collect or store your personal health information.

4.2 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Data is encrypted using iOS built-in encryption
  • The App is protected by your device's passcode/biometric authentication
  • Voice recognition processing is performed on-device only
  • Photos are stored in the App's sandboxed container

4.3 iCloud Backup

If you have iCloud backup enabled on your device, your App data may be included in iCloud backups. This is controlled by your device settings, not by the App. iCloud backups are encrypted and managed by Apple according to their privacy policy.

5. Information Sharing

5.1 No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 User-Initiated Sharing

The App allows you to share information only when you explicitly choose to:

  • Email session reports to your healthcare providers
  • Export data for your own records
  • Email supply orders to your nurse or dialysis center

These actions use your device's native email functionality. We do not receive copies of these communications.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, but since we do not collect or store your data on our servers, we have no access to your health information to disclose.

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 FDA GUDID Database

The App may connect to the FDA's Global Unique Device Identification Database (GUDID) to retrieve medical device information when you scan barcodes. These queries do not include your personal information.

6.2 Apple Services

The App uses Apple's native services:

  • Speech recognition (on-device processing)
  • Camera and photo library access (with your permission)
  • Email integration (using your device's mail app)

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • View all stored information within the App settings
  • Edit or delete any information at any time
  • Export your data for personal records
  • Delete all data by using the "Delete My Data" option in settings

7.2 Permissions

You can manage App permissions in your device settings:

  • Camera (for barcode scanning and photo documentation)
  • Microphone (for voice commands)
  • Speech recognition (for voice guidance)
  • Photo library (to save and attach photos)

8. Children's Privacy

Dial-Assist is not intended for use by individuals under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information through the App, please contact us, and we will provide guidance on deleting the data from the device.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing an in-app notification for material changes

10. Medical Disclaimer

Dial-Assist is an adherence tracking tool and is not a medical device. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always follow your healthcare provider's instructions and consult with them before making any changes to your treatment.

12. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where applicable
  • Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) privacy principles (note: as a consumer app storing data locally, HIPAA does not directly apply)
